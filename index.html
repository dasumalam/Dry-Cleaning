Find answers to the most common questions about our laundry and dry cleaning services.

Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours for regular service. However, we also offer express service with same-day turnaround for an additional fee. The exact timing depends on the type of garments and cleaning requirements.

Yes, we offer convenient free pickup and delivery services within our service areas. You can schedule a pickup online or via phone, and our team will collect your garments from your doorstep and deliver them back to you cleaned and pressed.

We use a variety of cleaning methods depending on the garment type and fabric. This includes traditional dry cleaning, wet cleaning, and specialized treatments for delicate fabrics. We also use eco-friendly cleaning agents that are gentle on fabrics but tough on stains.
